About Us

Crafting Excellence
Since 1999

Incorporated in 2008 as Window Grille Pvt. Ltd. — and rooted in a 35-year legacy — we are Kolkata's foremost manufacturer and exporter of architectural metalwork.

Under the leadership of Mr. Satya Narayan Gupta, a veteran in hardware and wrought iron, with the next-generation stewardship of Mr. Girish Gupta and Mr. Manoj Gupta, we unite traditional craftsmanship with contemporary engineering precision.

All merchandise is exclusively designed by our in-house specialists — ensuring every project resonates with artistry, structural integrity and lasting beauty.

Our Design Philosophy

We believe architecture is incomplete without the poetry of metalwork. Every curve, cutout and finish we apply carries intent — balancing the demands of structural permanence with the desire for lasting visual elegance. Our legacy is not merely in the products we make, but in the spaces they define: stairwells that command attention, balconies that frame skylines, and gates that set the first impression of a home. From wrought iron to laser-cut stainless steel, each material is chosen deliberately — and every weld, polish and powder coat is a quiet expression of craft earned over three decades.
