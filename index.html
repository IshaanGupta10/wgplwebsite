About Us

Crafting Excellence Since 2008

  • 2008

    Inception

    Window Grille Pvt. Ltd. began its journey in Kolkata, India, laying the foundation for its legacy in crafting premium architectural embellishments.

  • 2011

    Diversification

    With a commitment to innovation, the company expanded its product range to include a diverse array of ornamental grilles, railings, gates, and more.

  • 2015

    Advancement

    Embracing modern technologies and design trends, Window Grille Pvt. Ltd. transitioned to offer cutting-edge solutions tailored to meet evolving customer needs.

  • 2020

    Expansion Phase Two

    In line with its vision for growth, Window Grille Pvt. Ltd. embarked on its phase two expansion, further solidifying its position as a leader in the industry.
